so you want to know who i am.

i'm shorki. i'm a weirdo who loves technology — not in a clean, corporate way, but in the way that makes you stay up until 4am trying to get something to work just because it seemed impossible.

this site is my corner of the web. no templates, no themes, no following trends. just a page i built piece by piece, the way i wanted it. it's rough around the edges and i like it that way.

you'll find me across a bunch of platforms if you want to reach out. i'm on discord, twitter, steam, bluesky — the usual spots. or you can drop something in the guestbook. i read all of them.

thanks for stopping by. make some noise while you're here.
